I work on AI and Engineering for the Dutch Government, currently as Engineer in
the Bureau Architectuur Digitale Overheid (BADO), where I primarily work on
[RegelRecht](https://regelrecht.rijks.app) — an exploration into machine-executable
legislation. I also run [Axolotl Systems](https://axolotl.systems), where I provide
ML engineering and platform consulting services one day per week alongside my
government work, including working as ML Engineer at [Ditto](http://ditto.care/).
I hold a [PhD](/publications/schuth-phd-thesis-2016) from the University of Amsterdam
where I studied self-learning search engines. My

## Internships and Visits

*Cambridge, United Kingdom*<br>
**Research Intern** (2014)<br>
Collaborated with dr. Filip Radlinski and dr. Katja Hofmann for 12 weeks on
online evaluation of Bing, focusing on bringing interleaved comparisons closer
to AB testing. Published a [full paper at SIGIR2015](/publications/schuth2015predicting).

*Moscow, Russia*<br>
**Research Intern** (2014)<br>

and with Damien Lefortier, working on online learning to rank (bandit) algorithms
for freshness ranking.

#### Yahoo! Labs

*Barcelona, Spain*<br>
**Visiting Researcher** (2012)<br>
Collaborated with dr. Gianmarco De Francisci Morales in the group of dr. Aristides
Gionis on integrating a semantic linking project developed at the University of
Amsterdam into Yahoo!'s search-logs processing.
